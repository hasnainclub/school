Open Firearm Registration

This website is an unbiased tool to visualize and represent gun ownership in the United States.

This tool can be used to address the issues of gun violence in America through unbiased information. This website shows the location of registered firearm owners in your neighborhood, and provides information about the laws pertaining to each state reqarding legal ownership of firearms.

This information gives you vantage in decisions about the safety of you and your family.
